ðŸ”¹ What is BottomNavigationBar?

The BottomNavigationBar in Flutter is a bar at the bottom of the screen used for switching between different app sections or tabs.



ðŸ”¹ Key Components

items

List of BottomNavigationBarItems.

Each item has an icon and label.

currentIndex

Indicates the currently selected tab.

onTap

Callback triggered when a tab is tapped.

Typically updates currentIndex to switch content.

backgroundColor

Sets the color of the bottom bar.

selectedItemColor / unselectedItemColor

Colors for selected and unselected tabs.



ðŸ”¹ Why Use BottomNavigationBar?

Provides easy navigation across main sections.

Improves user experience by keeping tabs always accessible.

Maintains Material Design consistency.



ðŸ”¹ Common Use-Cases

Switching between Home, Search, Profile screens.

Quickly accessing important app sections.

Tabs with icons and text for visual clarity.
